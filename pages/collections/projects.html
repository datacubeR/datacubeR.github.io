Proof of Concepts

A proof of concept (PoC) is a realization of a certain method or idea to demonstrate its feasibility whose purpose is to verify that some concept has the potential of being used. A proof of concept is usually small and may or may not be complete. A pilot project is targeting a limited scope of the intended final solution. Wikipedia
